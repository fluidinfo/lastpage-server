lastpage.me

If you want to tell someone a URL, how do you normally do it? What's the absolute easiest possible way?

With lastpage.me it takes 2 mouse clicks.

  1. You click to record where you are
  2. Your friend clicks a link on their browser toolbar to jump there

Compare that to what you'd typically do during a phone conversation:

  1. Copy the URL of the page you're looking at in your broswer
  2. Switch to your email program
  3. Enter or the recipients email address
  4. Maybe write a Subject line
  5. Paste the URL into the mail
  6. Click send
  7. Go back to your browser
  8. Meanwhile the other person switches to their email program
  9. You both wait until they receive the email
  10. They click to view your email
  11. They click on the link in the mail

Use a URL shortener

URL shorteners can help, and make it feasible to read a URL aloud, but they're still more awkward than it should be. You have to go and create one each time, and every short URL you make is different. Once you've made a short URL, you still have to get the other person the sequence of random upper- and lower-case letters and numbers.
