On the phone, how do you normally share a URL?

  1. Copy the URL of the page you're looking at in your broswer.
  2. Switch to your mail program.
  3. Enter or find your friend's mail address.
  4. Maybe write a Subject line for the mail.
  5. Paste the URL into the mail body.
  6. Click send.
  7. Go back to your browser.
  8. The other person switches to their mail program.
  9. You both wait until they receive the mail.
  10. They click to view your mail.
  11. They click on the link in the mail.

Of course there are other ways: reading a complicated URL aloud, or finding a URL shortener and reading a randomized URL string, or maybe using some kind of instant messaging. That's all work. It can be so much simpler...

With lastpage.me sharing a URL takes 2 mouse clicks!

  1. Click to record the page you're on.
  2. Your friend clicks a browser bookmark to jump there.
